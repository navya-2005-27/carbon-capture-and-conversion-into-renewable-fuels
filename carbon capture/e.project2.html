Project 2:

1. Online Hub

Resource Library
  • Research Papers: Access to academic research on carbon capture, storage, and conversion.
  • White Papers & Technical Reports: Reports from industry leaders and agencies.
  • Guides and Tutorials: Step-by-step guides on technologies like DAC, CCS, etc.
  • Equipment Database: Catalog of materials and devices for carbon processes.
Knowledge Sharing and Collaboration Tools
  • Discussion Forums and Chat Groups: Topic-specific forums for collaboration.
  • Webinars and Workshops: Sessions led by experts on new technologies.
  • Collaborative Projects: Post project ideas and join real-world applications.
Simulation and Modeling Tools
  • Carbon Capture Simulation: Tools for simulating different capture technologies.
  • Chemical Reaction Models: Models showing carbon conversion into fuels.
  • Economic Feasibility Calculators: Cost models for evaluating technology viability.
Innovation and Funding Portal
  • Funding Opportunities: Listings for grants and government subsidies.
  • Open Innovation Challenges: Competitions for proposing solutions.
  • Case Studies: Success stories in carbon-to-fuel technology.
Data and Standards Repository
  • Data Sets: Access to CO₂ concentration data and lifecycle assessments.
  • Technical Standards: Guidelines for safety and quality control in conversion.
Networking and Partnership Opportunities
  • Directory of Experts: Searchable directory of researchers and institutions.
  • Mentorship Programs: Pairing of new innovators with experienced mentors.
  • Job Board: Listings for jobs and internships in relevant fields.
Carbon Calculator for Conversion Impact
  • Emissions Reduction Calculator: Tool for estimating emissions reduction.
  • Project Viability Dashboard: Calculates CO₂ reduction potential and fuel production.
